About Us

EconMe is a financial management website designed to assist users with tracking expenses and budgeting. It provides valuable insights through graphs and data that users might not easily access on their own.

The platform's key feature is its ability to visualize financial activities, helping users to understand their spending patterns and make informed decisions about their finances. EconME aims to simplify personal finance through user-friendly tools that enhance financial literacy and planning.
